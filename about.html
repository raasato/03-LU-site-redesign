Francine Miller

Francine Miller is the heart and color behind Fran's Flowers. Raised in San Francisco, Fran spent much of her childhood exploring vibrant neighborhoods with her grandmother and tending to their community garden. From an early age, she learned that flowers are more than a decoration, but a symbol of life, love, and hope. Those early experiences shaped both her creativity and sense of care for the community.

She opened the shop in 1995 with the simple belief that flowers bring joy and connection to the community. For many years, Fran has remained rooted in San Francisco's Mission District, nurturing relationships and cultivating beauty with every bouquet.
